Because of this, you are responsible for making sure your clients are comfortable with you storing their measurements, photos, and order details in ZikFash, in the same way you would already be responsible for how you store that information in a paper register or on your phone.

ZikFash is a tool to support your professional judgment, not a replacement for it. Part B — Privacy Policy

1. What Information We Collect

ZikFash collects and stores the following categories of information, entered by you as the User:

  • Your account information (name, contact details, shop information).
  • Client information you enter on behalf of your customers, including names, contact details, body measurements, style preferences, and order history.
  • Photos you or your clients provide, including style reference photos, garment images, and (for AI measurement features) body-reference images.
  • Usage & Analytics Information: We collect basic analytics (such as pages viewed, device type, and app performance) as you navigate between our marketing site and the app. We use a secure, first-party session identifier rather than intrusive third-party marketing cookies. This ensures your usage data remains strictly private and is used solely to improve ZikFash.

2. How Information Is Stored

ZikFash uses an offline-first design. Information is stored locally on your device (via on-device browser storage) and synced to our cloud database (hosted on Supabase) when you are connected to the internet. This allows the app to keep working without an internet connection while keeping your data backed up centrally.

3. How Information Is Used

  • To provide core app functionality (storing and displaying client/order records).
  • To power AI-assisted features, where relevant information (such as a style photo or measurement description) is sent to a third-party AI provider strictly to generate the requested output (e.g. a style preview, a detected measurement, or a search result), and is not used by us for purposes unrelated to providing that feature.
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the app over time.
  • To communicate with you about your account, app updates, or (once active) credit/billing matters.

4. Third-Party Services

ZikFash relies on third-party infrastructure and AI providers to deliver certain features, including cloud hosting/database services and AI/image-processing services used for style previews, measurement assistance, and reference image search. These providers process the specific data needed to fulfil each request (such as an image submitted for analysis) under their own data handling practices. We choose providers with reasonable security and privacy standards, but encourage you to avoid entering highly sensitive personal information about your clients beyond what is needed for their order.

5. Client Data: Your Responsibility as the User

Because your clients do not interact with ZikFash directly, you act as the data controller for the information you enter about them, and we act as a data processor providing the storage and tooling. You are responsible for:

  • Letting your clients know, in whatever way is appropriate for your business, that their measurements and photos are stored digitally.
  • Only entering information you have a legitimate, agreed basis to collect and store.
  • Removing client information if a client asks you to, using the app's deletion features.

6. Data Retention

Your information and your clients’ information is retained for as long as your account remains active, so that your records remain available to you. You may delete individual client records or your account at any time; deleted records are removed from our active systems, subject to reasonable backup retention periods.

7. Data Security

Requests relating to your own account can be made directly within the app or by contacting us. Requests relating to client data should first be directed to the tailor/shop who manages that client's record.

9. Children's Information

ZikFash is intended for use by tailors and shop owners operating a business, and is not directed at children.
