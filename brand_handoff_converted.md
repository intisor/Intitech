## __The Logo Mark — Karakul Cap__

The Intitech logo mark is derived from the Karakul cap — a traditional cap widely worn across Muslim and Central/West African communities\. Abdulawwal is recognized by his Karakul cap in crowds\. When he eventually starts content creation, the cap will be his visual trademark\.

__Mark description:__

- Front\-facing view — authoritative, looking straight at the viewer
- Plain black, no texture, no fluff — geometric trapezoid silhouette
- Folded rim as single clean curved line at the crown
- Bold "INTI" in a contrasting color written on the cap face — the cipher made visible
- Single vertical seam line — minimal, precise

__Logo states:__

- Mark Light — parchment \(\#c9b99a\) cap on light background
- Mark Dark — dark cap \(\#1c1a13\) with sediment border on void background
- Mark Ember — dark cap with ember \(\#c8612a\) stroke — for hover/active states

The full lockup = Mark \+ INTITECH wordmark \+ "I build what's needed\." tagline\. File: intitech\-logo\-system\.svg
