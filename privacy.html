Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

ChatLasso Extension

ChatLasso is built with your privacy as the absolute highest priority. Our philosophy is simple: Your data is yours.

  • No Telemetry: We do not track, collect, or monitor your usage.
  • 100% Local Execution: The extension parses web pages directly inside your browser tab. We have no backend servers.
  • No External APIs: Your AI conversations never leave your device. All captured data is processed locally and downloaded directly to your hard drive.
  • No Personal Information: We do not ask for, process, or sell any personal data (e.g., names, email addresses, payment info).
